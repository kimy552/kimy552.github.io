The False Mirror

  • The False Mirror is a surrealist oil painting by René Magritte that depicts a human eye framing a cloudy, blue sky. In the depiction of the eye in the painting, the clouds take the place normally occupied by the iris.
  • Artist: René Magritte
  • Period: Surrealism
  • Created: 1928
  • Medium: Oil paint
